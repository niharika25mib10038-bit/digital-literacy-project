As a Digital Ambassador I made a page called "Digital Literacy" using Canva. My main goal was to make something that would really help my peers understand the side of the internet.

To make sure everyone could understand the information I broke it down into three parts:

* Defining Digital Literacy: I wanted to make it clear that being good at digital things is not just about knowing how to use a computer. Digital Literacy is about using the internet and tools in an responsible way. Digital Literacy is really important for all of us.

* Online Safety: This part gives people tips on how to stay online like making strong passwords and keeping their online information clean.

* Professional Communication: I made a guide, for sending emails, which includes things like making sure the subject line is clear and the tone is professional. This is a part of Digital Literacy and it can really help people in their careers. Digital Literacy is something that we all need to learn and practice every day.
